About me

Before I begin I'd like to apologize for this little ruse that I've set up for a potential employer. It is true that I'm at the verge of ending university and the picture that can be seen in this sections shows Warsaw University of Technology. By now you've probably expected me to study IT or anything connected with it.. Well, my faculty is Administration and Social Sciences and before you leave I'd like to assure you that it's a great place for future web designer! My bachelor thesis was about project management and tools to minimalize risk in the organisation. It also covered popular these days Agile methodologies.

As for the hobbies and personality I'm calm and diligent person. I enjoy swimming, riding a bike and shooting with a bow. I'm sure that my love to archery came from fantasy books that I simply adore. Other hobbies would be history, Japanese culture and classical music.
